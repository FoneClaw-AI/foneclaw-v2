# Supported Devices

FoneClaw is designed for Android 9.0 and newer.

## Android brands

FoneClaw is an independent Android product and is not limited to one phone manufacturer. Supported Android workflows may be used across brands such as:

- Samsung
- Xiaomi
- Google Pixel
- OnePlus
- OPPO
- Realme
- Motorola
- Vivo
- Sony
- Nokia
- Other Android 9.0+ devices

## Requirements

- Android 9.0+
- Microphone permission for voice input
- Accessibility Service permission for supported phone control workflows
- Notification permission for notification summaries
- Other permissions depending on enabled workflows

## Notes

Specific app behavior can vary by Android version, manufacturer skin, user permissions, app UI, and regional availability.
